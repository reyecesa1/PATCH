Why PATCH Is Perfect For Adventures

PATCH is designed specifically for people on the move who can't always rely on internet connections.

Works Completely Offline

No internet? No problem! PATCH runs entirely on your device, so you can use it anywhere - from mountain tops to remote forests.

Lightweight & Fast

PATCH is designed to be small and efficient, so it doesn't drain your battery or slow down your device while you're traveling.

Remembers Everything

PATCH keeps track of your conversations, notes, and preferences, so it gets better at helping you the more you use it.

Adventure-Ready

Whether you're planning routes, identifying plants, or just need travel advice, PATCH is your perfect hiking companion.

Privacy Focused

Since everything happens on your device, your conversations and data stay private - no one else can access them.

Quick Setup

Get PATCH running in minutes, not hours. Simple installation means you can start using it right away.

How PATCH Works

PATCH uses lightweight AI models that are perfect for offline use on portable devices.

TinyLlama - The Lightweight

Super small and fast, perfect for quick answers and everyday tasks when you're on the move.

Get PATCH Ready For Your Next Adventure

Setting up PATCH is quick and easy - you'll be ready for your next trip in no time.

1

Install Ollama

Ollama is the engine that runs AI models on your computer. It's free and easy to install.

# Download from https://ollama.ai
# Choose your operating system and install
2

Download PATCH's Brain

Once Ollama is installed, download the lightweight AI model that powers PATCH.

# Open terminal/command prompt and run:
ollama pull tinyllama

This only takes a few minutes and downloads everything PATCH needs to work offline.

3

Get the PATCH Interface

Download the PATCH console that gives you the cool terminal interface to interact with.

# Download from our website
# This gives you the adventure-ready interface
4

Start Your Adventure!

Run PATCH and start having conversations with your new AI hiking companion.

# Double-click the PATCH icon
# Or run: ./patch-console

That's it! You're now ready to take PATCH on your next adventure.
